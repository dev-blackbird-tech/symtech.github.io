What We Do

Symtech provides expert solar installation services for efficient,
eco-friendly, and sustainable energy solutions.

Site Assesment and System Design

Evaluates the location's suitability for solar installation, including sunlight exposure and roof condition, then designs a customized solar power system tailored to the site's specific needs

Installation Setup

Installs solar panels, inverters, and other necessary equipment, ensuring proper alignment and secure mounting. Connects the system to the electrical grid or battery storage, and tests its functionality.

Permission and Compliance

Handles the necessary permits and ensures the solar installation complies with local regulations and codes. This involves coordinating with local authorities and utility companies to secure approvals and facilitate a smooth installation process.

Maintainance and Support

Provides ongoing maintenance services to ensure the solar system operates efficiently. This includes cleaning panels, inspecting and repairing components, and offering customer support for any issues or upgrades.
