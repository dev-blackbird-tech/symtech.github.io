Why Should You Install a CCTV System?

It is increasingly important for a company/home to have reliable and effective security systems, such as access controls, an alarm system and video surveillance cameras or a CCTV system (Closed Circuit TV).

CCTV systems allow you to remotely and automatically monitor your facilities' insides, either with video monitors and specialized personnel, or in the case of SMEs through a mobile phone (smartphone) or a computer.

Video surveillance cameras can be connected to your alarm system, and allow remote viewing of monitored spaces with high image quality.

Digital recorders, meanwhile, allow you to store recordings for a predetermined period of time, and perform searches and filtering in the video database, applying artificial intelligence algorithms for facial recognition or license plate recognition, among other functions advanced.

The existence of these security systems constitutes a deterrent that helps prevent theft, intrusions and other crimes, and allows the detection of undue behavior by staff members, customers or visitors. Increase the safety of your clients and employees, and it is a tool that can serve as evidence in a judicial process against any criminal behavior.
